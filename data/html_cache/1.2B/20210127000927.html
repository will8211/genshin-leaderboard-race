Game8 Japan's Overall Ranking

Constellation levels listed below are considered the ideal level for that character's role.
For example:
• Ningguang gains a small support passive at C4, but no further impactful support passives past C4. Thus Nigguang is added to Support B Tier at C4.
• Xinqiu is already a high ranked Sub-DPS and is considered at his best at C4 and above.

Ranking Critera and Role Breakdown

Tier List Overview Criteria

・Characters that perform above and beyond others in their respective roles.
・Characters that easily become core party members.
・Characters with high performance in their respective roles, but don't push the limits interms of Talents and Constellations.
・Characters with solid performance in their respective roles.
・Characters that shine in specific party compositions.
・Functional characters that will be quickly replaced due to their talents, stats, etc.
・Characters with limited combat utility.
・Characters more suited for exploration.

Role Breakdown

Role Function
DPS ・The main damage dealer of the party.
・Characters with consistent high damage output.
Sub DPS ・Uses Elemental Skills or Bursts to provide Elemental Reactions to support main DPS.
・Charactesr with flexible Elemental Skill or Burst utility.
Support ・Provides healing and sustainablity to the party.
・Provides buffs and debuffs.

Tier List Criteria

Elemental Skill and Burst Utility is Highly Consisdered

Ranking Factor
  • Elemental Skills and Bursts that can be taken advantage of even after swapping.
  • Elemental Reaction trigger effectiveness of Elemental Skills and Bursts.
    		•

    Simply speaking, Elemental Skills and Bursts that are easy to use are rated highly. This list considers how easily Elemental Reactions can be triggered as well as if the skill effects continue if characters are swapped. As Elemental Reactions are a major part of how combat functions in Genshin Impact, this list also considers how Elemental Skills and Bursts factor into party composition and synergy as well.

    Character Versatility is Valued Greatly

    Ranking Factors
  • Whether or not a character is dependant on others for full functionality.
  • Strengths as a stand-alone character.

    • This list rates characters and their versatility on an individual basis rather than as part of specific party compositions. Characters that can smoothly clear Domains and easily handle Bosses solo are highly valued.
