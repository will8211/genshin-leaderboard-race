Tier List Criteria

Elemental Skill and Burst Utility is Highly Consisdered

Ranking Factor
  • Elemental Skills and Bursts that can be taken advantage of even after swapping.
  • Elemental Reaction trigger effectiveness of Elemental Skills and Bursts.
    		•

    Simply speaking, Elemental Skills and Bursts that are easy to use are rated highly. This list considers how easily Elemental Reactions can be triggered as well as if the skill effects continue if characters are swapped. As Elemental Reactions are a major part of how combat functions in Genshin Impact, this list also considers how Elemental Skills and Bursts factor into party composition and synergy as well.

    Character Versatility is Valued Greatly

    Ranking Factors
  • Whether or not a character is dependant on others for full functionality.
  • Strengths as a stand-alone character.

    • This list rates characters and their versatility on an individual basis rather than as part of specific party compositions. Characters that can smoothly clear Domains and easily handle Bosses solo are highly valued.
